About

I'm a tattoo artist based in Hội An, Vietnam. I draw funny, illustrated things for a living and I started this because I was losing something — the pure joy of drawing just for paper.

I also started it because I got scared of what's happening to art. So I did the most analogue thing I could think of. I found an envelope. And I started mailing things to strangers.

No algorithms. No feed. Just mail.

FAQ

Questions.
Answered.

When does my first envelope ship?
Sign up by the 15th of the month and your first envelope ships by the 25th of that same month. Subscribe after the 15th and you'll receive your first envelope the following month.
Is there tracking?
No tracking — it keeps costs down and honestly, waiting without knowing is more fun. You'll get a message from us on shipping day so you know it's on its way.
What if it doesn't arrive?
If your envelope hasn't arrived after 6 weeks, contact us. We'll resend. It's rare but it happens and we'll always make it right.
Can I cancel?
Yes, anytime. No awkward emails, no guilt trips. Cancel through your Stripe customer portal before your next billing date.
Can I send it as a gift?
Absolutely. At checkout add the recipient's name and their address. It ships directly to them every month.
Can I reply to the letter?
Yes and we love this. The return address is on the envelope — write back, send a drawing, send anything. All replies are read. Some end up on Instagram (with permission).
What is the Golden Floating Boob?
Every month one random envelope contains something extra and original. There's no pattern and no announcement. You'll know it when you find it.
Is the art different every month?
Yes. New print, new postcard, new sticker, new bookmark, new fortune strip every single month. No repeats.
