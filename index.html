Can I try just one envelope before subscribing?
Yes — the "Try one envelope" option gets you a single one-off envelope for $18. No subscription, no commitment. If you love it, subscribe from the next month.
When does my first envelope ship?
Sign up by May 4th and your first envelope ships May 8th. After that, sign up by the 15th of each month and your envelope ships by the 25th. Delivery takes 2–3 weeks worldwide.
Is there tracking?
No tracking — it keeps costs down and honestly, waiting without knowing is more fun. You'll get a message from us on shipping day so you know it's on its way.
What if it doesn't arrive?
Delivery takes 2–3 weeks worldwide. If your envelope hasn't arrived after 6 weeks, contact us. We'll resend. It's rare but it happens and we'll always make it right.
Can I cancel?
Yes, anytime. No awkward emails, no guilt trips. Cancel through your Stripe customer portal before your next billing date.
Can I send it as a gift?
Absolutely. At checkout add the recipient's name and their address. It ships directly to them every month.

A tiny sealed envelope inside the main one — but it's not for you. Instructions inside will tell you what to do with it. Give it to someone. Leave it somewhere. Pass it on. Don't open it yourself. That's the whole point.
