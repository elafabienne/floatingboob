good questions

Before you ask.

Can I try just one envelope before subscribing?

Yes — the "Try one envelope" option gets you a single one-off envelope for €16. No subscription, no commitment. If you love it, subscribe from the next month.

When does my first envelope ship?

Sign up by the 15th of the month and your envelope ships at the end of that month. After the 15th, you're in the following month's send. Delivery takes 2–3 weeks to Europe and 4–5 weeks to USA, Australia and the rest of the world.

Is there tracking?

No tracking — it keeps costs down and honestly, waiting without knowing is more fun. Envelopes ship on the 25th of the month, so you know when it's on its way.

What if it doesn't arrive?

Delivery takes 2–3 weeks to Europe and 4–5 weeks to USA, Australia and further destinations. If your envelope hasn't arrived after 6 weeks, contact us. We'll resend. It's rare but it happens and we'll always make it right.

Can I cancel?

Yes, anytime. Just email ela@floatingpost.com before the 15th of the month and we'll sort it out before the next send. No awkward emails, no guilt trips.

Can I send it as a gift?

Absolutely. At checkout add the recipient's name and their address. It ships directly to them every month.

What is the mystery envelope?

A tiny sealed envelope inside the main one — but it's not for you. Instructions inside will tell you what to do with it. Give it to someone. Leave it somewhere. Pass it on. Don't open it yourself. That's the whole point.

What is the Dó paper postcard?

Every month one random subscriber receives an original Vietnamese art postcard on Dó paper — traditional handmade paper crafted in Vietnam for over a thousand years. Ela found it in Hội An. It's slipped into one lucky envelope with no announcement. You find out when you open it.

What is the Golden Boob?

Every month one random envelope contains a golden boob sticker. Find it and you win a hand-drawn portrait in Floating Boobs style — just you, you and your partner, your family, your best friend, whoever you want. Send Ela a photo and she draws them. Original, signed, one of a kind. Warning: it might look funny. It's a real Floating Boobs piece — not a selfie.

Is the art different every month?

Yes. New print, new postcard, new sticker, new fortune strip every single month. No repeats.
